Hi there!


Hi! I am Tawsif. I am Data Scientist living in Brooklyn, New York. I was born and raised in Bangladesh. I moved to Canada in 2013 for my masters. Then I moved to the Bay Area in 2019 to work at Uber, and later to New York in 2020 to join Spotify.

Over the years, I have worked on a variety of problem spaces, wealth management, credit cards and loyalty, retail and most recently on the digital consumer space.

I primarily focus on inference problems and mostly work with product and engineering teams. I take a consultative approach to my work where I try to bridge the gap between theory and business.

When I am not working, I am usually at a coffee shop reading a book or strolling around taking pictures with my phone.
